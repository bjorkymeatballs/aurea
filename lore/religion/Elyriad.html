ANNOTATED BY
MIRTHALAS WYNNAETH

The narrator of the text is referred to as Álláris by all other characters. Curiously, "Álláris" is a title not a name, meaning 'teacher' or 'guide'. The identity of the narrator is not explicitely stated by the text. Based on the fact that the narrator does possess gnosis, it is likely that they are either Elyria, El, or some other emanation of the Divine.
